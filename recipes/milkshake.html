Vannila Milkshake Recipe

Description

A vannila milkshake is a delicious and very easy to make recipe. You could add chocolate, berries, peaches, etc.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vannila extract

Steps

Below are the steps on how to make a vannila milkshake:

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Blend ice cream, milk, and vannila extract together in a blender until smooth.
  3. Pour into glass and serve.
